# 🎮 PARANOIA

### *The ultimate party game for starting drama, roasts, and absolute chaos with your friends.*

Welcome to **Paranoia**, a clean, dark-themed web game designed to turn your next hangout into a hilarious web of secrets. The rules are simple: you get a secret question, you name someone in the room out loud, and a coin flip decides if everyone finds out *why* you chose them. 

👉 **[Click here to play the live game!](https://your-live-demo-link.com)**

---

## 👀 What's inside?

*   **Easy Lobby Setup:** Throw everyone's names into the list and you're good to go.
*   **Pick Your Vibe:** Toggle different decks depending on who you're playing with:
    *   😇 *Icebreaker & Friendship:* Wholesome and safe for family or new friends.
    *   😂 *Funny & Party:* Pure chaos, jokes, and light roasts.
    *   🌶️ *Call-Out:* Dramatic and boundary-pushing.
    *   🔞 *spicy Mode:* Explicit stuff for spiced up nights (not actually 18+).
*   **Privacy Shields:** Built-in "pass the device" screens so sneaky eyes don't ruin the surprise.
*   **RNG Suspense:** A satisfying coin-flip screen to break hearts or save secrets in real time.

## 🛠️ The Tech Stuff

Kept it simple and fast so it loads instantly on anyone's phone or laptop:

*   **Frontend:** React / Next.js
*   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (with a nice, custom dark mode)
*   **Hosting:** Vercel / GitHub Pages

---

## 🕹️ How to Play (The Quick Version)

1. Pass the device to the player named on the screen. 
2. That player taps to reveal and read the secret question *silently*.
3. They **say their answer OUT LOUD** to the room (just a player's name—no context!).
4. Hit **Flip the Coin**:
    *   🪙 **HEADS:** The question is revealed! Everyone gets to see exactly why you put them on blast.
    *   🪙 **TAILS:** Safe! The question stays a secret and you move to the next round.
